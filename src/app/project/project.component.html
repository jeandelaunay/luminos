The project


The aim of our project is to reduce light pollution in cities. This will not only protect the ecosystem but also reduce the budget that cities spend on electricity. Public lighting can represent almost 80% of the energy budget for small cities and 20% for large cities like Paris.

The solution


Thanks to the raspberry pi technology. We plan to equip each light point in a city with a motion detector. This will allow the light intensity to be adjusted according to the passage of citizens. This will allow cities to save up to 30% of their annual budget in terms of public lighting. Here is a video demonstrating our solution.

The application


In the near future we also plan to produce a dashboard allowing cities to have a better follow up of the light consumption. This monitoring will allow them to know the breakdowns but also to know better where people pass and thus to set up a better management of safety at night.
